Money for lead paint removal and home improvements

Welcome to the initial screening for lead paint removal and home improvements. There are seven questions we will ask to get you started. If you qualify or if you do not qualify and request to be contacted, we will contact you within two weeks.

Lead paint removal program

You can apply for as much as a $10,000 grant for lead removal costs, such as new windows.

Home repair program

Hennepin County offers deferred loans up to $30,000 at low or no interest to qualified recipients for home repairs and maintenance.
